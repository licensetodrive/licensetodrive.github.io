Lessons

A ‘lesson’ usually last for 1.5 hrs although this is adaptable and flexible to the needs of the student.

All lessons are individually planned to suit the ability and progress of the student and start with:

  • Recap – Progress made the previous lesson,
  • Objectives – What we are aiming to achieve in the lesson,
  • Briefing – How we meet the objectives,
  • Feedback – How the lesson went, did we meet the objectives.

Pass Plus

I am registered to offer the Pass Plus driving scheme. I am a Grade A instructor. This is a mini course that offers six modules with a minimum of six hours tuition, focussing and further developing driving skills in the following areas:

  • In town
  • In all weathers
  • On rural roads
  • At night
  • On dual carriageways
  • On motorways

The course is aimed primarily at newly qualified drivers but can be accessed by anyone with a full licence. There are no formal exams to pass but ongoing assessments are carried out by the instructor and all modules must be successfully completed to the required standard. Insurance companies can offer a discounted premium to drivers who have successfully completed the course but check with your own company. The Pass Plus course is offered at £195 in students vehicle or £230 in instructor vehicle. Further information from the gov.uk website.
